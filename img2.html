Pyaar vo khanani hai,
jisse hume banani hai,
saath pakad k haath hume bitani hai,
dur ho ya pass, har pal zindagani hai !


Aap iss tarah humari jaan ban gaye.. ye maloom na hua,
yuh rukh ke badalne ka isshara bana,
jab aap aaye toh kuch naya mehsoos hua.


Tum mere jeevan ka sangeet ho,
meri dhadkan ka ahsaas ho,
meri har khushi ka kaaran,
aur mera pyaar ho.


Aankhe dhundhe anokhe se tere mukhade ko,
na jane kyu pukaare mere dewaane ko.
Shayad mil gya hai jaaneman tu mujhe,
bekarar hu tujhe dekhne ko...
